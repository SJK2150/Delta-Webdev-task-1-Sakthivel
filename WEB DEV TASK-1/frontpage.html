/\TASK 1/\

デルタフォースへようこそ

Rules:

Canon Shooting: The canon is responsible for shooting at the Titan.

Time Limit: Ensure that the action does not exceed 45 seconds.

Canon Restrictions: The canon cannot shoot bullets that land on the outer edges of the boar

Ricochet Behavior: A ricochet can reflect bullets from both sides. A semi-ricochet can only reflect bullets from one side. If a bullet reaches the other side, the semi-ricochet will be destroyed.
